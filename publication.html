Research Report

NDYA Raises Concern Over Bank Delays in ‘Form A’ Processing for Nigerians Abroad

NDYA reports growing concerns from Nigerians in the diaspora over significant delays in banks’ processing of ‘Form A’ transactions. These delays are affecting students, workers, and families who rely on timely foreign exchange approvals..

Key Findings:
  • Prolonged bank delays are disrupting tuition payments
  • Students and workers are facing financial strain due to untimely access to approved funds.
  • Immediate improvement in processing efficiency is necessary to ensure timely financial support for the diaspora community.
