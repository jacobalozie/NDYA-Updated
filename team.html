Our Team

Meet the passionate individuals driving NDYA's mission forward

Dedicated to Youth Empowerment

Our team consists of passionate professionals, volunteers, and advisors committed to empowering Nigerian youth in the diaspora. Together, we work to create opportunities, foster leadership, and build a strong community.

Amb. David Victor Alozie

Founder & Executive Director

Amb. David Victor Alozie founded NDYA in 2018 with a vision to empower Nigerian youth in the diaspora. With years of experience in youth development, he leads our strategic direction and global expansion.

Comr. Ameh Samson

Deputy NDYA

Comr. Ameh Samson oversees all NDYA programs and initiatives. With a background in education and community development, he ensures our programs effectively meet the needs of Nigerian diaspora youth and align with our mission.

Bar. Lilian Ezebuihe

Liaison Officer

Bar. Lilian develops and maintains strategic partnerships with organizations, corporations, and institutions. Her extensive network and negotiation skills have been instrumental in expanding NDYA's reach and impact.

Comr. Jacob Apollos

National General Secretary

Comr. Jacob ensures the smooth operation of NDYA's day-to-day activities. With expertise in project management and logistics, he coordinates our events, volunteers, and administrative functions across all chapters.

Bar. Onyinye Dike

Legal Adviser

Bar. Onyinye provides legal guidance to the organization, reviews official documents, ensures NDYA's activities comply with relevant laws, and advises the leadership on legal risks and best practices.

Bar. Eze Chidubem Esq

Legal Adviser II

Bar. Eze supports the organization by conducting legal research, preparing documentation, and offering clear counsel on policy and governance matters. He helps interpret regulations, ensures proper legal procedures are followed, and assists the team in making informed, compliant decisions.

Comr Ifenla Oligbinde

Director of Projects and Programs.

Oversees the planning, execution, and evaluation of all organizational projects and programs to ensure impact, efficiency, and alignment with NDYA’s mission.

Comr. Charles Stephanie Chiamaka

Treasurer

Provides financial oversight, maintains accurate records, and supports the executive team with transparent and responsible financial management.med, compliant decisions.

Comr. Balogun Olaide Adebuka

General Secretary.

Ensures effective internal and external communication, manages documentation, and keeps the organization’s operations well-organized.

Comr. Anumba Williams Linda Uche

Financial Secretary.

Assists in managing the organization’s finances by recording transactions, tracking income and expenses, and supporting financial reporting.
