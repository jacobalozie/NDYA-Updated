About NDYA

Our Story

Amb. David Victor Alozie serves as the President of NDYA. Under his leadership, NDYA has advocated for the welfare of Nigerians abroad, defended student rights, and called for support from national institutions to address diaspora concerns.

He is passionate about youth empowerment and creating platforms for Nigerians in the diaspora to connect, share experiences, and influence positive change both at home and abroad. Through his guidance, NDYA continues to strengthen its role as a bridge between diaspora youths and national development initiatives.
